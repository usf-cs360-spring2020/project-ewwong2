About

This site aims to dive deeper into the courses offered at the University of San Francisco. Although I am a student at the University of San Francisco, I never bothered to look depper into the statistics of the school beyond the pamphlet given to me in freshman year.


Over the course of my undergraduate years, I have always had issues with registration whether it be limited course capacity, prerequisite, hold, or restriction that I needed to get resolved. While this website will not and cannot be the solution to that problem, I am hoping to explore different ways to present information that could have helped me back then. As a graduate student now, I have much less issues with registration, but perhaps the ideas in the site could be used to either gain insight on the course system or help with registration.
